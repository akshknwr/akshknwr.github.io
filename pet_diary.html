Welcome to PetDiary

A digital diary for your pets health record that you can check anytime just with your finger tips.

How to use

  • When you open for first time, it will have blank home screen and on top right you will see New button
  • Tap the new button which will open new screen where you can enter pet details
  • To get started, all you can enter is name of your pet if you're in hurry and edit rest later.
      • For this guide we will enter all details
    • edit button to update the image, and select the image you want for your pet
    • Enter name of the pet
    • Select DOB of pet
    • Add breed of Pet
    • Enter the weight of pet
    • When you enter weight, you will notice the weight recorded date pops up, and enter the weighed date
    • Save the profile on top right button
  • Now you will see your pet on home screen, to add more you can follow the same procedure above
  • Tap on your pets name and you will see pets detail where you can add more details
      • To add more details,
    • click edit on top right corner
    • Enter any Allergies, Health Issues or medications and save
      To add Vaccination, Weight, Grooming details
  • Tap on Setting tab on the bottom of screen
  • Once Settings tab opened, you can turn on all the options that you want on pet details page
  • once turned on, go to Home tab and select pet
  • On vaccination, tap and add new
  • Enter the details of vaccination and save
  • Vaccination detail will appear on detail page
  • Follow the same for rest of details including weight, grooming, worming, others
