Welcome to Daisy Calendar

Daisy Calendar is a simple, easy to use calendar that gives you traditional wall calendar look and feel.

How to use

  • Swipe Up to go next month
  • Swipe Down to go previous month
  • Click on Date to see selected date

Changing Theme or settings

  1. Long press just above the images on the binder
  2. A dock view appears on the bottom
  3. Tap on settings
  4. select theme and choose your theme from the list
  5. Tap on calendar from the dock view and see the new theme
  6. Tap on any empty spaces i.e. Image on calendar and dock will disappear giving more space
